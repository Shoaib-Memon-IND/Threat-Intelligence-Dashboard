WELCOME TO
MALWARE DETECTION
PROJECT

We investigate malware detection, where a deep learning approach is developed to classify malware in an executable file. In contrast to the traditional malware detection systems based on signature, our method is based on static detection to reveal malicious and benign files. We present a effective deep learning method to classify malicious software. The proposed method makes use of a one-dimensional convolutional neural network 1D-CNN due to the nature of the portable executable file. Empirically, the efficiency of the proposed methods is demonstrated in comparison to the state-of- the-art methods on three datasets. The results of the experiment demonstrated that the proposed approach outperforms several classification models and provides a practical, reliable, and rapid mechanism for detecting malware in an executable file.
